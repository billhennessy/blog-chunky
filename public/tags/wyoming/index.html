Wyoming Republican County Conventions

January 5, 2008

Former governor Mitt Romney has won the Wyoming Caucuses. Romney has 6 of the 12 delegates up for grabs. Two other delegates are split between Thompson and Duncan Hunter. With 4 delegates to be decided, that leaves Romney with a lock. Nonetheless, if Thompson could pick up 3 of the remaining 4, it would be a banner day for the true conservative voice. The Wyoming GOP has an Excel spreadsheet showing the candidate favored in each county.
