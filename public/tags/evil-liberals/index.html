Why Did I Serve?

September 7, 2007

It breaks my heart to write these words. I spent 11 years in the U.S. Navy. I thought the long hours, the humiliation, the lousy pay, and missing the 1980s was worth it. I thought my country appreciated our work. Apparently, I was wrong: Zogby Poll: 51% of Americans Want Congress to Probe Bush/Cheney Regarding 9/11 Attacks; Over 30% Seek Immediate Impeachment If I believed this poll be accurate, I really wouldn&rsquo;t want to be an American any longer.
