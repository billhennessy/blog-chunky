Quid Est Veritas

January 4, 2020

Quid Est Veritas? Sometimes I think Pontius Pilate gets a bad rap. Sure, he was ultimately the slithery politician who sentenced Jesus to death, but he was also the only person on earth who offered Jesus something of a defense. When the Jews first presented Jesus to Pilate, Pilate told them to try Jesus themselves. But the Jews argued that they could not lawfully put Him to death (John 18:31).
