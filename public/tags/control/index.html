Free Forever Through Stoicism

July 16, 2017

&ldquo;Some things we can control, some we can’t. We can control our attitudes, opinions, goals and desires – choices of our own. We can’t control health, wealth, fame or power – things we can’t have by choosing them.&rdquo; —Epictetus Freedom begins with understanding what we control and we do not. Yet very few people even begin to consider this essential dichotomy. Even in the complete absence of &ldquo;administration&rdquo; as Tocqueville observed in early 19th century America, no one was free who failed to understand Epictetus&rsquo;s control dichotomy: some things are under our control, some are not.
