It's About the Destination, Not the Journey

December 31, 2019

Jerry Garcia Was Wrong Jerry Garcia of Grateful Dead was famous for saying &ldquo;It&rsquo;s about the journey, not the destination.&rdquo; Garcia was wrong. There are two possible destinations: Eternal perfection. Eternal misery. It&rsquo;s all about the destination. The destination is all that matters. The journey matters only insofar as it determines the destination. If you know and pursue the right destination, the journey will be difficult, often painful, often lonely, often frustrating.
