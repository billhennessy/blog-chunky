I Cheat

June 21, 2016

A friend and colleague paid me a huge compliment recently. A compliment I didn&rsquo;t deserve. He said something about trying to mimic my writing style. That&rsquo;s an ego-booster, for sure, but, well, I don’t really have a writing style. I cheat. See, I experiment with every writing tool I hear about, and I stick with the ones that measurably improve my writing. Especially when they work as I write (because I hate editing and revision, as you can tell).
